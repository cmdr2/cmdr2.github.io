Got [Freebird](https://freebirdxr.com) working on a Mac (with Blender 5.2 alpha). Used the new [ox](https://github.com/ox-runtime/ox) runtime (v0.6 pre-release) to simulate OpenXR on a Mac, and worked through the bugs to get Freebird running.

So if someone ever creates an OpenXR-compatible headset for the Mac, Freebird will run on it!
