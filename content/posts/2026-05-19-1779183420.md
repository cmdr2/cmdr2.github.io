[ox](https://github.com/ox-runtime/ox) proved its worth again today. It was much faster and easier to iterate and develop using ox to preview changes (and click things using the virtual controller). I used a real headset only for final verification, and some minor tweaks (like fixing z-fighting). It's really cumbersome otherwise to keep donning a real headset on/off for every code change, especially when iterating rapidly on a feature.
